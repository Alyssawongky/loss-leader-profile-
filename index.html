This is not a personality test.

The Loss Leadership DNA is a connection-focused framework. It identifies how you make decisions, communicate, respond under pressure, and learn — then translates that into practical guidance you can use immediately.

Your result is a personalised report with two lenses: one for you, and one for your manager. No jargon. No labels. Just a clear picture of how you work best — and how the people around you can bring out more of it.
